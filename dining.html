Hello Rufus - Shayla

For a while in the winter we had an infamous 8th roomate, Rufus the mouse. "Oh my god, we should'nt even speak his name." Rufus kept the weirdest hours of any mouse to ever live. He would only be active from 2pm-7pm despite Google assuring us that mice were nocturnal. For a while, I didn't believe Rufus was real despite Shayla announcing herself with a scream any time she entered a room to scare him off. I decided to brave the first floor where he lived to do some work at the kitchen table when all of a sudden Shayla appears and tells me to look down. "Ummm, Rufus was under your chair." She immediately ran away, leaving me to fend for myself.


Friendsgiving - Emily

"It was so wholesome." Friendsgiving was our last hurrah together before everyone scattered for the actual holiday. Emily secured the recipe for her grandma's mac and cheese, which was actually the best mac and cheese I've ever had. We decorated the table and took lots of Instagram stories of clinking glasses and the spread of food. It's not that often that we all have the time to get together like this and eat as a house so having excuses to do so always feel special.


Poppers - Liz

Liz is our resident holiday enthusiast. For every season change, you can bet she's going to TJ Maxx to find some new decorations. For the Christmas season, she found some holiday gnomes and Christmas poppers to open at our last dinner together. "They're so fun!" The prizes inside ranged from a mini yo-yo to a tiny roll of tape, all equally unique and weird. "They even came in handy" that night when we needed a piece of tap and a pencil. Though the prizes were promptly abandonded within 24 hours of popping them open, they still made for a fun evening.
